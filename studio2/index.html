My Hometown

I am from Santa Rosa where we are not only surrounded by wineries but also was home to Charles M. Schulz. Here, Schulz spent years writing his Peanuts Comics inspired by his surroundings. There are now Peanuts Character Statues all over Santa Rosa.

Yosemite Valley Camping

First time camping out in tents surrounded by giant sequoia trees. Explored the sites on their free buses and hiked around the park as well. From this day, my goal has been to return to Yosemite and take the 14 mile hike up Half Dome!

NBA

As a fan of basketball, I bought the Kings Pass during my second year at UC Davis. One day, I got selected for two updated tickets and was not only moved to the lower level but also recieved two gift cards from Great Clips, their sponsors!

Orlando

I played basketball for 9 years and when I got the chance to go to Orlando, I was thrilled. I loved looking at Michael Jordan's huge statue and all the hall of fame hand printed basketballs that were in Universal Studios. I also escaped the heat by enjoying one of their many waterparks.
