Best Practices for Form Design

Studio 1.1 ~ January 31, 2023

As designers, we have to account for the amount of work users want to put into forms. For example, when filling out surveys, I look to see how long it is before deciding if I am to complete it at the moment or come back to it later. I agree with the "less is more" theory. Simplifying the form whether that's putting everything within one column or categorizing sections will help users have a faster completion time. I don't like filling out forms that feel endless. Some don't include any progress bar or indication of the survey coming to an end which diminishes my experience.

Additionally, it helps to differentiate any requirements whether that's special formatting or marking required fields. Previously, I would use placeholder text but now I realize this isn't the best way to provide hints as the text disappears once the user starts typing something.
